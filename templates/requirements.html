Distinguished Active Requirements

  • Tutoring (2 hrs/week from Week 3 to Week 9) and/or EMCC
  • Two events hosted by TBP (i.e. infosessions, socials, Engineering Futures, competitions, etc.)

Once members have completed the requirements to become distinguished actives they are eligible to purchase a white stole to wear at graduation (see picture). Members will receive $5 off their stole for completion of the requirements in subsequent quarters. All members who complete the distinguished active requirements for a given quarter will be invited to attend the next TBP banquet.

House Points

Each initiation quarter, candidates and officers will be divided into four houses - Tau, Beta, Pi, and Epsilon - and will compete throughout the quarter to earn points for their house. The house that earns the most points by the end of the quarter will win the honor of Top House, as well as a special prize.

Ways to Earn House Points:
  • First three houses to turn in Professor Interview/Resume (1st: 25, 2nd: 10, 3rd: 5)
  • Best candidate signature book (1st: 25, 2nd: 10, 3rd: 5)
  • Best candidate quiz score (1st: 25, 2nd: 10, 3rd: 5)
  • Attending info-sessions (5 per event)
  • Complete extra tutoring hours (1 per hour, max 5 per week)
  • Extra community service events (5 per event)
  • Attend extra social events (5 per event, 50 if entire house attends extra social)
  • Win games at social events (1st: 25, 2nd: 10, 3rd: 5)
