TBP House Cup Competition Rules

This is a friendly competition held among the candidates and members of Tau Beta Pi during initiation quarters. The goal is to foster meaningful friendships and experiences between members as well as to add some competitive flavor to the quarter. Individuals can earn points in the following ways:

{% include "house_points_snippet.html" %}

Ways to Earn House Points:

Completing the Initiation Process  
Tutoring at your scheduled hours (7 weeks) – no points for makeup 2 points/week
Community Service 6 points
TBP Event (or Professor Interview: 4 points) 6 points
Turn in Signature Project 6 points
Social Events (2) 3 points/event
Bent Polish 2 points
Turn in Resume on time 2 points
Passing Candidate Quiz on first try (70%+) 2 points
Attend Candidate Sorting/Meet and Greet/Engineering Futures 2 points each
Total: 50 points

Extra Stuff/Ways To Make Up Points		  
Extra tutoring (max 2 hours per week) 2 points/hour
Extra community service 12 points/event
Extra TBP event 12 points/event
Attending an extra social event (3+) 6 points/event
100% on candidate quiz 8 points
90%-99% on candidate quiz 6 points
80%-89% on candidate quiz 4 points
Best signature project - first place 18 points
Best signature project - second place 12 points
Best signature project - third place 6 points

Social Events		  
If 100% members of house attend an event/social 3 points/attendee
If 70%+ members of house attend an event/social 2 points/attendee
If 50%+ members of house attend an event/social 1 points/attendee

Resumes		  
1st house to turn in all resumes 3 points/person
2nd house to turn in all resumes 2 points/person
3rd house to turn in all resumes 1 points/person

Distinguished Active Points		  
Tutoring at your scheduled hours (7 weeks) – no points for makeup 2 points/week
Extra tutoring (max 2 hours per week) 4 points/hour
Attend TBP event (2) 3 points/event
Attending an extra TBP event (3+) 6 points/event
