TBP House Cup Competition Rules

This is a friendly competition held among the candidates and members of Tau Beta Pi during initiation quarters. The goal is to foster meaningful friendships and experiences between members as well as to add some competitive flavor to the quarter. Individuals can earn points in the following ways:

Ways to Earn House Points:

Completing the Initiation Process
Tutoring at your scheduled hours (7 weeks) – no points for makeup 2 points/wk
Community Service 6 points
Attend social event (2) 2 points/event
Turn in professor interview, resume on time 6 points
Turn in signature book on time 6 points
Bent polish 2 points
Passing quiz on first try (70%+) 2 points
Total: 40 points
Extra stuff/ways to make up points
Extra tutoring (max 2 hours per week) 5 points/hr
Extra community service 15 points/event
Attending an extra social event (3+) 5 points/event
100% on membership quiz 15 points
90%-99% on membership quiz 10 points
80%-89% on membership quiz 5 points
Social events
If 100% members of house attend an event/social 3 points/attendee
If 70%+ members of house attend an event/social 2 points/attendee
If 50%+ members of house attend an event/social 1 points/attendee
Interviews and Resumes
1st house to turn in all interviews and resumes 6 points/person
2nd house to turn in all interviews and resumes 4 points/person
3rd house to turn in all interviews and resumes 2 points/person
Distinguished Active Points
Tutoring at your scheduled hours (7 weeks) – no points for makeup 2 points/wk
Extra tutoring (max 2 hours per week) 5 points/hr
Attend social event (2) 2 points/event
Attending an extra social event (3+) 5 points/event
