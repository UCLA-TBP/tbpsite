Rube Goldberg Competition

Saturday, February 2, 2013
10AM-3PM
SAC

Registration is now closed! To be placed on the waitlist, please fill out the form here.

Teams of 5-8 have 3 hours to design, build, and test a Rube Goldberg machine. A Rube Goldberg machine is a machine that performs a simple task in the most complicated manner possible. All of the teams' machines are required to perform the same task, which in the past has been pouring hand sanitizer and knocking down bowling pins.

TBP provides all of the materials to the teams and each team is given the same supplies. Materials generally consist of household items, such as sponges, popsicle sticks, and rulers, but also include motors, switches, and magnets.
